This new release supports multiple languages, and includes a larger internal English dictionary.
The app itself can be translated and major words can be created in other languages using latinized alphabet.
The words can be listed in any number or all of the available languages.
The definitions of the words will be translated into the app's current language.
It currently includes Spanish but later will automatically include French, German, etc..
The app also randomly selects 500 words to show if word count greater than 500.
